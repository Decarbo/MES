Mechanical Engineering Society B.I.T Sindri is a technical society in B.I.T Sindri which focuses on the wholesome development of the students. Mechanical Engineering Society B.I.T Sindri conducts many events, seminars, guest lectures, workshops throughout the year.

With the motto of holistic development, Mechanical Engineering Society focuses on the personality devlopment and enhancement of technical skills of students in B.I.T Sindri.
